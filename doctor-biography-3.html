Dr. Nasrin Niggar

Gyanaechologist

Dr. Nasrin Niggar is a highly experienced Gyanaechologist with a passion for helping patients lead healthier lives. He has over 8 years of experience in the field and is dedicated to providing the best cardiac care to his patients.

Dr. Nasrin Niggar completed his medical degree from a prestigious institution and pursued further specialization in cardiology. His expertise includes diagnosing and treating various heart conditions, including heart disease, arrhythmias, and more.

Patients describe Dr. Nasrin Niggar as a caring and compassionate physician who takes the time to explain medical conditions and treatment options. He is committed to promoting heart health and well-being in the community.
